# WYA Landing Page Storyboard

## Target Audience
- New visitors discovering WYA for the first time
- Returning users who signed out and need a quick refresher

## Page Narrative Flow
1. **Hero** – Establish WYA’s core promise in Kenya, highlight AI + community angle, primary CTA to create account, secondary CTA to explore events.
2. **Trust Signals** – Quick stats or partner logos proving momentum (events hosted, users served, communities onboarded).
3. **Feature Highlights** – Three-card grid explaining `Discover Events`, `Request Custom Events`, and `AI Assistance`.
4. **Personalization Snapshot** – Explain tailored recommendations, include carousel/screenshot of AI suggestions.
5. **Community Stories** – Quote or story snippets from organizers/attendees.
6. **How It Works** – Three-step walkthrough from sign-up to attending.
7. **Mobile Experience (Optional)** – Showcase responsive/mobile UI if assets available.
8. **FAQ Preview** – Answer top concerns: availability, pricing, support.
9. **Final CTA Banner** – Reinforce sign-up with supporting secondary CTA for business/event organizers.

## Section Copy Drafts

### Hero
- **Headline:** “Find the Pulse of Kenya’s Events—Powered by WYA”
- **Subheadline:** “Discover curated experiences, request custom gatherings, and let AI match you with moments you’ll love.”
- **Primary CTA:** `Get Started`
- **Secondary CTA:** `Browse Live Events`
- **Tertiary CTA (text link):** `See AI in Action`

### Trust Signals
- **Copy:** “Trusted by 12k+ attendees, 350 organizers, and dozens of cities across Kenya.”
- **Supporting text:** “From Nairobi to Mombasa, WYA keeps event lovers in the know.”
- **CTA:** none; optional `View Success Stories`.

### Feature Highlights
- **Discover Events:** “Explore music, culture, sports, and tech gatherings curated for the Kenyan scene.”
- **Request an Event:** “Have an idea? Launch requests, gather interest, and partner with organizers to bring it to life.”
- **AI Assistance:** “Tell us what you’re into. WYA’s AI recommends events, suggests collaborations, and keeps you ahead of the crowd.”

### Personalization Snapshot
- **Heading:** “Tailored to Your Vibe”
- **Body:** “Answer a few prompts or sync your interests and our AI creates a personal feed of events, alerts, and recommendations.”
- **CTA:** `Try AI Recommendations`

### Community Stories
- **Heading:** “By the Community, For the Community”
- **Quote placeholder:** “WYA helped us fill our festival within days.” — *Nairobi Arts Collective*
- **Secondary snippet:** “I discovered night markets I never knew existed.” — *Achieng, Food Enthusiast*
- **CTA:** `Read More Stories`

### How It Works
- **Step 1:** “Create your profile in minutes.”
- **Step 2:** “Choose interests or request your own event.”
- **Step 3:** “Get matched, book tickets, and share the experience.”
- **Support text:** “We keep you updated with smart notifications and social features.”

### Mobile Experience
- **Heading:** “WYA Goes Where You Go”
- **Body:** “Stay in the loop with real-time alerts, ticket wallet, and community chat on your phone.”
- **CTA:** `Join the Waitlist` (if app still in beta) or `Download App` (if available).

### FAQ Preview
- **Questions:**
  - “Is WYA free to use?” – “Yes, browsing and requesting events is free. Paid events show clear pricing.”
  - “Can I host my own event?” – “Absolutely. WYA connects organizers with attendees, tools, and sponsors.”
  - “Where does WYA operate?” – “We’re available across Kenya with more regions rolling out soon.”

### Final CTA Banner
- **Headline:** “Ready to Experience Kenya’s Event Scene Differently?”
- **Primary CTA:** `Create Your Free Account`
- **Secondary CTA:** `Book a Demo for Organizers`
- **Footer note:** “Need help? Chat with our team or explore AI assistance.”
